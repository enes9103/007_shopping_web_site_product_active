Meet the Owners

David and Sasha met on a backpacking adventure in Nepal. John, a former sheep shearer, went on the trip to get some clarity about his next adventures in life. Sasha — a Peloton instructor — went on the trip to explore the landscape in Nepal.

While on the trip, David and Sasha realized that they had one common love — the love for hiking and the love for being outdoors.

Returning back to their home state of Brooklyn, John and Jane hatched a plan to launch a retail store centered around their passions.

While David handled the sourcing of goods and customer service, Jane focused on the web + e-commerce side of the business, building this website in the best design platform on the web — Webflow!

In 1989, David and Sasha Doe officially launched Acme Outdoors, the premiere store for outdoor enthusiasts around the state.

To this day, David and Sasha return to Nepal yearly to ensure that they never forget their roots and where they came from.
